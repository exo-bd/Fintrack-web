Why it's safe to hand over this much detail

Your financial data stays yours.

Local-first storage

Your ledger lives on your device, encrypted, before anything ever touches the cloud.

Lock with fingerprint or face

Every time you open the app, biometric authentication stands between your money and anyone else.

One sealed space per account

Switching accounts never leaks another account's numbers — each gets its own isolated data.

Your profile stays private

Your name, photo, and personal details are private by default. In Community, you choose what to share — your numbers are never shown unless you choose to share them.

AI insights see a summary, not your data

Only an aggregated monthly summary — income, expenses, savings, category percentages — is ever analyzed. No individual transaction, merchant name, or account number leaves your account, and no third-party AI company is involved.
