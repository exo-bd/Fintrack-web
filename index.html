Why it's safe to hand over this much detail

Your financial data stays yours.

Local-first storage

Your ledger lives on your device, encrypted, before anything ever touches the cloud.

Lock with fingerprint or face

Every time you open the app, biometric authentication stands between your money and anyone else.

One sealed space per account

Switching accounts never leaks another account's numbers — each gets its own isolated data.

SMS stays on your device

Messages are read on-device only to detect transactions — the raw text is never uploaded or shared.
