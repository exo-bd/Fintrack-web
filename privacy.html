Introduction

FinTrack ("FinTrack", "we", "our", "us") is a personal finance app for Android, developed under the package com.fintrack.app. This policy explains what information the app accesses, why, and how it is stored. It applies to anyone who installs or uses FinTrack.

FinTrack is built around a simple idea: your financial data should help only you. Wherever possible, information is processed on your device rather than sent anywhere.

We process personal data on the basis of your consent, the performance of our agreement with you, and our legitimate interest in providing the app's core features. FinTrack is operated from Bangladesh and used in Bangladesh and elsewhere, so this policy is written to apply globally, while also honoring specific rights granted under frameworks such as the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), and Bangladesh's Personal Data Protection Ordinance, 2025 — summarized in Regional disclosures.

By creating an account or enabling a feature that requires a permission (such as SMS access or the camera), you consent to the related data use described in this policy. You can withdraw that consent at any time by disabling the permission or deleting your account — see Account & data deletion.

Information we collect

FinTrack collects only what it needs to run your ledger. We treat your financial data as sensitive information and apply additional care in how it is stored, accessed, and shared:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and profile photo, when you sign in with Google or create an account, via Firebase Authentication.
  • Financial data you create — transactions, budgets, categories, debts, recurring bills, and notes, whether entered manually, detected from SMS, or scanned from a receipt.
  • SMS content — read on-device to detect bank and mobile-banking transaction alerts. FinTrack extracts the amount, date, and merchant/sender pattern needed to create a ledger entry; it does not upload or store the raw message text.
  • Camera and photos — receipt images you choose to scan or photos you choose to attach, processed to extract amounts and dates.
  • Voice input — audio captured only while you actively use voice entry to add a transaction by speaking; it is converted to text on-device and not retained as an audio file.
  • Biometric data — handled entirely by your device's operating system to unlock the app. FinTrack never receives or stores your fingerprint or face data.
  • Gamification data — points, streaks, and reward progress generated from your in-app activity.
  • Device & diagnostic data — basic technical information (device model, OS version, crash logs) used to keep the app stable.

How we use your information

  • To create and maintain your transaction ledger, budgets, debts, and recurring bills.
  • To sync your data securely across your own devices when you're signed in.
  • To send budget alerts, bill reminders, and reward notifications you've enabled.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or technical issues.
  • To improve app stability and performance.
FinTrack does not use your financial data, SMS content, or receipt images to build an advertising profile, and does not sell this data to anyone.

Permissions we request

Android requires apps to declare each permission up front. Here is what FinTrack asks for and why:

You can review or revoke any of these at any time from Android Settings → Apps → FinTrack → Permissions. Revoking a permission only disables the related feature; it does not delete data you've already saved.

Storage & security

  • Local-first: your ledger is stored on your device first, in an encrypted local database, before anything syncs to the cloud.
  • Cloud sync: when signed in, data syncs to Cloud Firestore under your own Firebase account, governed by per-account access rules so one account can never read another's data.
  • Sensitive fields: credentials and tokens are kept in encrypted secure storage provided by the Android operating system, not in plain text.
  • Account isolation: on a shared device, switching accounts does not expose another account's transactions, points, or settings.

Account & data deletion

You can permanently delete your FinTrack account and all associated data at any time:

  • In the app: go to Settings → Account → Delete Account, and confirm.
  • From the web, without the app: visit our account deletion page and submit a request — useful if you've already uninstalled FinTrack.

Once verified, we permanently delete your account and the personal data tied to it — we do not just deactivate or freeze it. We may retain a limited subset of data where the law requires it (for example, billing records for tax purposes, or logs needed to investigate fraud or abuse); any such retention is described in Retention policy below.

Third-party services

FinTrack relies on a small set of trusted providers to operate:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Messaging) — account sign-in, data sync, and push notifications.
  • Google Sign-In — optional one-tap sign-in using your Google account.
  • Google AdMob — displays ads that help keep the free tier of FinTrack running.
  • Google Play Billing — processes premium subscription purchases; FinTrack never sees your card details.

Each provider processes data under its own privacy terms in addition to this policy.

International data transfers

FinTrack is based in Bangladesh, but our service providers — particularly Google Firebase and Google Cloud — may store and process data in other countries, including the United States and other regions where Google operates infrastructure. If you are located outside the country where your data is processed, your information may be transferred internationally.

Where required by applicable law (for example, the GDPR for EEA/UK residents), such transfers rely on appropriate safeguards, such as standard contractual clauses or other mechanisms recognized as providing an adequate level of protection. By using FinTrack, you understand that your data may be processed outside your country of residence as described here.

Advertising

FinTrack shows ads through Google AdMob on free-tier screens. Depending on your region and consent choices, ads may be personalized or non-personalized. You can manage ad personalization in your device's Google Settings, and remove ads entirely with a premium subscription.

Data sharing & disclosure

We do not sell your personal or financial data. We share data only with the service providers listed above, as needed to operate the app, or when required by law.

Retention policy

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active, so that your ledger, budgets, and history remain available to you. If you delete your account (see Account & data deletion), associated personal data is permanently deleted, except for narrow categories we may be legally required to keep — such as billing records, or logs needed to investigate fraud, abuse, or security incidents — which are kept only as long as necessary for that purpose.

Children's privacy

FinTrack requires users to be at least 18 years old (see our Terms & Conditions), which is above the digital consent age in every jurisdiction we're aware of, including the EU/UK's GDPR (typically 13–16) and the US's COPPA (13). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Your rights

Wherever you are located, and subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information, most of which you can edit directly in the app.
  • Delete your account and associated data at any time — see Account & data deletion.
  • Restrict or object to specific processing, such as turning off SMS-based detection, voice entry, or ad personalization, while continuing to use the rest of the app.
  • Withdraw consent for any permission-based feature at any time from Android Settings, without losing access to your previously saved data.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Contact us, or use our Support page. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law, or otherwise within a reasonable time.

Regional disclosures

Depending on where you live, you may have additional or more specific rights under local law. The sections below summarize how some of the most common frameworks apply to FinTrack; they supplement, and do not replace, the general rights described above. If your country isn't listed, contact us — we'll honor requests consistent with the privacy rights available to you locally wherever we reasonably can.

European Economic Area & United Kingdom (GDPR / UK GDPR)

  • Our legal basis for processing is, depending on the feature: your consent (for permission-gated features like SMS access, camera, or voice entry), the performance of our contract with you (for core ledger and sync features), and our legitimate interest (for security, fraud prevention, and service improvement).
  • You have the rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and to object to processing, as well as the right to withdraw consent at any time without affecting processing already carried out.
  • You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

California, USA (CCPA / CPRA)

  • The categories of personal information we collect are described in Information we collect above — including identifiers, financial information, commercial information, and audio/visual information you provide (such as voice input or receipt photos).
  • We do not sell personal information for money. Displaying personalized ads through Google AdMob may be considered "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising under the CCPA/CPRA; you can opt out by turning off ad personalization in your device's Google Settings, or by contacting us to opt out of personalized ads entirely.
  • You have the right to know, delete, and correct your personal information, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising any of these rights.
  • An authorized agent may submit a request on your behalf with proof of authorization.

Bangladesh (Personal Data Protection Ordinance, 2025)

Bangladesh's Personal Data Protection Ordinance, 2025 recognizes you as the rightful owner of your personal data, requires explicit consent before collection or use, and classifies financial information as sensitive data warranting enhanced protection — consistent with the practices described throughout this policy.

Other countries

If your country provides additional statutory privacy rights not explicitly listed here — for example, under Canada's PIPEDA, Brazil's LGPD, India's DPDP Act, or Australia's Privacy Act — we will honor requests consistent with those rights to the extent required by applicable law. Contact us using the details below to exercise any right available to you locally.

Governing law

This policy is governed by the laws of the People's Republic of Bangladesh, where FinTrack is operated from. Any dispute arising from this policy will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Dhaka, Bangladesh. This does not limit any mandatory consumer-protection or data-protection rights you have under the law of your own country of residence — for example, under the GDPR for EEA/UK residents or the CCPA for California residents — which continue to apply to the extent applicable law requires.
