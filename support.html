Contact us

For anything FinTrack-related — bugs, billing questions, feature requests, or data requests — email us and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

We typically reply within 2–3 business days.

Delete your account & data

You can request full deletion — even without the app installed

Deleting the app from your phone does not delete your account or your data from our servers. Use one of the options below to remove everything permanently.

Option 1 — In the app: open FinTrack → Settings → Account → Delete Account, then confirm. This immediately starts the deletion process.

Option 2 — From the web (no app required): email us at the address above with the subject line "Delete my FinTrack account" from the email address associated with your account, including:

  • The email or phone number you used to sign in to FinTrack
  • Confirmation that you want your account and all associated data permanently deleted

Once we verify the request, we permanently delete your account profile, transactions, budgets, debts, recurring bills, reward progress, and any synced receipt images — we do not deactivate or freeze the account, we remove it. This is usually completed within [X business days — set your real turnaround time]. We may retain a narrow set of data where law requires it (for example, billing records); see our Retention policy for details.

If you only want specific data removed (for example, just your receipt images) rather than your whole account, mention that instead in your email and we'll action that narrower request.

Frequently asked questions

Can other users see my financial data through Community?+

No. Only your display name and anything you actively choose to post or share (like a streak or a milestone) is visible to others in Community. Your account balances, transactions, and budgets stay private unless you explicitly share a number yourself.

How do I add a new account (cash, bank, mobile wallet)?+

Go to Accounts → Add Account, choose a type (cash, bank, mobile wallet, or custom), give it a name, and set a starting balance. You can add as many accounts as you track and switch between them anytime.

How accurate are AI Insights?+

Once a month, AI Insights compare a summary of your activity against your own past months to highlight trends — they're a starting point for reflection, not financial advice. If an insight looks off, it's usually because of a miscategorized or missing transaction earlier in the month; correcting that will improve next month's insight.

Does my data get sent to an AI company for Insights?+

No. Only a small monthly summary — your total income, expenses, savings, and category percentages — is used, never your individual transactions or account details. That summary is analyzed by a language model we run ourselves, not by OpenAI, Google Gemini, or any other external AI provider. See How AI insights work in our Privacy Policy for the full breakdown.

How do I cancel my premium subscription?+

Open the Google Play Store app → tap your profile picture → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → FinTrack → Cancel. Your premium features remain active until the end of the billing period you already paid for.

I deleted the app — is my account gone too?+

No, uninstalling only removes the app from your phone. Your account and data remain on our servers until you explicitly request deletion — see Delete your account & data above.

Can I export my reports as PDF or Excel?+

Yes — go to Reports inside the app and choose Export. You can generate a PDF for sharing or printing, or an Excel file if you want to work with the numbers yourself.

Can I export my data?+

Yes — contact us through Support and we'll provide a copy of your account data in a portable format.

I'm outside Bangladesh — will FinTrack still work for me?+

Yes. Every feature — accounts, budgets, debts, recurring bills, receipt scanning, reports, AI insights, and Community — works anywhere. FinTrack just happens to default to Bangladeshi Taka (৳) and includes bKash, Nagad, and Rocket as ready-made mobile wallet account types, since that's where most of our users bank.

I'm in the EU/UK or California — do I have extra rights?+

Yes. EEA/UK residents have rights under the GDPR (including access, erasure, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority), and California residents have rights under the CCPA/CPRA (including the right to know, delete, and opt out of personalized-ad sharing). See Regional disclosures in our Privacy Policy for details, or just contact us — we honor these rights regardless of where the request comes from.

Permissions, explained

FinTrack asks for a few Android permissions, each tied to a specific feature, never bundled together by default. You can grant or revoke any of them individually from Android Settings → Apps → FinTrack → Permissions at any time. For the full breakdown of what each permission does and why, see the Permissions table in our Privacy Policy.

Report a problem

Found a bug, a miscategorized transaction pattern, or something that just looks broken? Email us at the address above with:

  • A short description of what happened and what you expected instead
  • Your device model and Android version, if known
  • A screenshot, if it's a visual issue

This helps us reproduce and fix issues much faster.
